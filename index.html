A commitment to conservation and responsible tourism has been an integral part of the construction of our properties and our daily operations ever since we opened our first camp in Nairobi National Park in 1999. Sanctuary Retreats selects projects based on their potential to create long-lasting relationships with the communities in the areas in which we operate. This also creates awareness among different ethnic groups around the world concerning the Endangerment of the Fauna and Flora.
